The Waumap project archives a 10-year record of major commercial buildings near Wausan-ro, the location of Hongik University and presents this information as interactive ‘street-view’ images and motion typography.

Hongik University Station boasts the largest floating population in Seoul, where almost 100,000 people visit or pass through every day. For this reason, the area experiences a severe increase in rent and housing costs year after year, and the buildings in the downtown area are demolished and rebuilt regularly. Changes in the stores reflects the desire and needs of the people living in the area of that particular year. The project allows us to determine in some form, which products have been produced and consumed by the people in the area over the past decade.

This work is conceived and made by Project-Rebel9.
